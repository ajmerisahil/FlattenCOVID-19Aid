Basic Symptoms

COVID-19 typically causes flu-like symptoms including a fever and cough. In some patients - particularly the elderly and others with other chronic health conditions - these symptoms can develop into pneumonia, with chest tightness, chest pain, and shortness of breath.

Respiratory droplets may also be produced during breathing out, including when talking, though the virus is not generally airborne.
